Mathematics Holiday Homework

1. Biomass accounts for approximately 14% of all fuel consumption in the world. What fraction of all fuel consumed is biomass?

$$ \begin{alignedat}{1} Sol^n:\;&Percentage\;of\;biomass\; = \;14\% \;of\;all\;fuel\;consumed\; \\ \end{alignedat} $$ $$ \begin{alignedat}{2} \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; &\therefore Fraction\;of\;all\;fuel\;consumed\;being\;biomass\; &&= \;\frac{14}{100} \\ & &&= \;\frac{7}{50} \\ \end{alignedat} $$ $$ \begin{aligned} Ans:\;\frac{7}{50}\;of\;all\;fuel\;consumed\;is\;biomass. \\ \end{aligned} $$

2. In a developing country, most of their biomass fuel comes from wood (1/4), crop residues (1/3) and animal waste (1/8). What is the total fraction, decimal, and percentage amount from these three sources?

$$ \begin{alignedat}{2} Sol^n:\;&Biomass\;fuel\;obtained\;from\;wood\;=\;\frac{1}{4} \\ &Biomass\;fuel\;obtained\;from\;crop\;residues\;=\;\frac{1}{3} \\ &Biomass\;fuel\;obtained\;from\;animal\;waste\;=\;\frac{1}{8} \\ &Total\;amount\;of\;biomass\;obtained\;from\;all\;these\;re sources\;&&=\;\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{3}+\frac{1}{8} \\ & &&=\;\frac{6+8+3}{24} \\ & &&=\;\frac{17}{24} \\ & &&or\;0.708\overline 3 \\ & &&or\;70.8\overline 3\% \\ \end{alignedat} $$ \begin{aligned} Ans:\;Total\;biomass\;obtained\;from\;wood,\;crop\;residues\;and\;animal\;waste\;is\;\frac{17}{24}\;or\;0.708\overline 3\;or\;70.8\overline 3\%\;of\;the\;total\;biomass.\; \end{aligned}
